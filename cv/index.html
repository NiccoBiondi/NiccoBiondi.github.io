Summary A Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Florence (MICC) developing advanced machine learning and computer vision systems, with applications in multimodal learning and biomedical domains. My primary research focuses on representation learning, specifically designing backward-compatible and stationary representations to enable seamless model updates in large-scale systems. My work has been published in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, CVPR (highlight), and IEEE TPAMI.
